## Member Meetings

### Touchpoints

Asynchronous, on-demand discussions led by the TODO Group Steering Committee and surfaced whenever new trends emerge in open source or open source AI. Rather than running on a fixed calendar, Touchpoints are scheduled around Steering Committee availability and triggered by what's actually moving in the ecosystem (a new policy, a shift in tooling, an emerging practice) 

### All Hands
Live, semi-annual gatherings open to all TODO Group members and Ambassadors, supported by TODO Group staff at the Linux Foundation. Each edition is run twice in two complementary time slots so practitioners across APAC, EMEA, and AMER can join at a reasonable hour. All Hands are the moment when the member community comes together to review program progress and hear regional updates from Ambassadors and member organizations.

## Other Calendars of Interest

TODO is one of the pillars of the *open source management and operations best practices* ecosystem at the Linux Foundation. We work with sister projects like SPDX (Software Bill Of Material automation tooling and best practices), OpenSSF (Security best practices, training and tools), OpenChain (Process Management Standards), FINOS (best practices for financial services) and CHAOSS (Project / Community health and sustainability metrics). For more info, visit the [LF Management & Best Practices Portal](https://www.linuxfoundation.org/projects/management), where communities of *Best Practice* converge.
