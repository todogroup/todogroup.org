The TODO Group is pleased to announce the [results of the 2025 Steering Committee election](https://todogroup.org/blog/sc-elections-2026/), selecting members who will serve from **2026 to 2028**.

## Election Overview

This year’s election included **three elected seats** and **one appointed seat**. A ranked-choice voting system was used. Below is a summary of head-to-head matchups:

- **Ashley Wolf** — won against all candidates  
- **Stephen Augustus** — loses to Ashley Wolf (12–6)  
- **Brittany Istenes** — loses to Ashley Wolf (17–3); loses to Stephen Augustus (14–5)  
- **Fernando Correa** — loses to Ashley Wolf (13–7); loses to Brittany Istenes (11–8)  
- **Zhiqiang Yu** — loses to Ashley Wolf (15–5); loses to Fernando Correa (11–7)  
- **Jan van den Berg** — loses to Ashley Wolf (16–4); loses to Zhiqiang Yu (10–8)  
- **Christian Taylor** — loses to Ashley Wolf (16–4); loses to Jan van den Berg (9–7)  
- **Priyanka Ojha** — loses to Ashley Wolf (18–2); loses to Christian Taylor (9–6)

## Elected Members for 2026–2028

Following the ranked results, the three highest-ranking candidates have been re-elected:

- **Ashley Wolf** (GitHub)  
- **Stephen Augustus** (Bloomberg)  
- **Brittany Istenes** (Fannie Mae)

## Appointed Seat for 2026–2028

In addition to the three elected seats, this year included one **appointed seat**.  
According to the TODO Group charter, the current Steering Committee appoints this seat from the remaining pool of nominated candidates.

The appointed member for the 2026–2028 term is:

- **Fernando Correa** (Mercado Libre)
