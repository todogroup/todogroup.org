{{< intro >}}
The OSPO Book Working Group develops and maintains the OSPO Book, a community-curated reference for organizations adopting and scaling open source management practices.
{{< /intro >}}

## What we work on

The group writes, edits, and reviews chapters covering OSPO foundations, governance, compliance, community engagement, tooling, and emerging topics. Contributions come from practitioners across industries, and content is released openly under a permissive license so any organization can adapt it for internal use.

- **Repository**: [ospology/ospo-book](https://github.com/todogroup/ospology/tree/main/ospo-book)
- **Mailing list**: [WG-ospo-book-project](https://lists.todogroup.org/g/WG-ospo-book-project)

New contributors are welcome. Open an issue or PR on a chapter you'd like to improve, or join the mailing list to follow ongoing work and review cycles.
