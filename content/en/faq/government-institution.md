---
title: As a government institution, can I join the TODO Group?
weight: 70
---

The TODO Group welcomes any government institution willing to collaborate on practices, tools, and other ways to run successful and effective open source programs, as well as to engage in discussions with experienced OSPO professionals.

Please, take a look to the [TODO Group Overview Presentation](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1p4dhx0Dg8fZDO8yzp7nWC2r5WHyVH-jjSQM59lkKLdo/edit?usp=sharing) to learn more about membership benefits.
